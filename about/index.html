Who Am I?

My name is Josh Girvin, and I’m a 22 year old (as of April 2013) software developer in Brisbane, Australia. I’ve done all sorts of stuff in the web industry since I was 17, including running my own business; building a start-up, and contracting/consulting work.

Nowadays, I live with my girlfriend and work for a data company in Brisbane City. I have decided that I want more experience in the industry before I try my hand again at running my own business/startup.

I have extensive experience with PHP (preparing to do my Zend cert this year, which I’m actually quite looking forward to!), HTML/CSS (of course), and dabble in about a dozen other languages. Lately, I’ve started getting involved in the Elementary OS project, mainly working on third-party applications.

I also used to be a radio presenter on a community station here in Brisbane, Switch 1197AM which is something I am going to get back into again this year. I am very involved in the local music scene, and absolutely adore the bands that spring up from the relatively small city.

My contact details (email, social networks and the like) are at the bottom of the page. I am currently on the lookout for a new position; ideally something involving a medium-sized team of great developers that I can learn from. Let me know :)

– Josh
