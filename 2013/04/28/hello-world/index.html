Hello, World!

28/04/2013

Hi all! Welcome to my – Josh Girvin – blog. It’s running on my own VPS, with Jekyll powering the blog itself. I’ve started blogs on and off throughout the years, but due to getting involved with Elementary OS’s development lately, I thought it would be prudent to post up some ramblings on FOSS, Software Engineering, and Living in Brisbane Australia – as well as whatever else takes my fancy.

I’m a 22yo Software Engineer, working at InfoView in Brisbane, Australia. I work on web applications by day, but by night I hack away at applications on the Linux Desktop. Check out the About page to find out more about me.

Now, you’ll notice there are no comments. There is however a Send to HackerNews link: If you think it’s worth discussing, either tweet me (@girvo) – or send it to HackerNews and comment there.

Enjoy!
